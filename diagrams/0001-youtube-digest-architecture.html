Key decisions

  • Discovery: RSS with UULF prefix (zero quota, filters Shorts). Fallback to search.list (100 units) if RSS fails.
  • Quota budget: videos.list at 1 unit/video (batch 50). 10 channels · 5 videos/day = 50 units/day out of 10,000 free.
  • Transcripts: yt-dlp --write-auto-subs --skip-download. Auto-update yt-dlp in cron script to track anti-bot patches (24–48h lag).
  • Preprocessing: Strip VTT codes → remove filler words → punctuation restoration (pysbd) before chunking.
  • Chunking threshold: <20k tokens = single call. >20k = 3k fixed chunks + synthesis. No overlap.
  • State: SQLite WAL mode. Status column: pending → summarised → delivered → error. Prune rows older than 30 days.
  • Telegram: HTML parse mode. Consolidated message. Suppress link previews. Split by channel if >4,096 chars. Silent on zero-video days.
  • MVP defers: parallel sub-agents, Whisper fallback, relevance scoring, duration/view count enrichment.
